# Licence decision required before final release

The current public repository contains an all-rights-reserved review notice that prohibits copying, modification, distribution, and reuse. That notice is difficult to reconcile with a repository presented as a reproducible research workflow.

The authors should make an explicit final choice:

- code: a standard open-source licence such as BSD-3-Clause or MIT;
- author-created documentation and tables: CC BY 4.0 is commonly used;
- third-party source data: retain the original source terms and attribution rather than relicensing them.

This release candidate does not select a licence on the authors' behalf. Do not delete the current rights notice until the replacement licence and the treatment of third-party data have been agreed.
